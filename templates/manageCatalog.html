To Import a Track
  • Click on Admin button below
  • Click on "albums" table
  • Click "add album" in the upper right corner
  • 'Pub date' is the date the album was published, e.g. 11/5/2019
  • Fill out the form and click "Save"
  • Click the link to the "catalog" in the upper right corner
  • Click "created bys"
  • Input album and artist
  • Click "Save"

  • If you want to add the artist who created the album:
    • Click the link to the "catalog" in the upper right corner
    • Click "featured ins"
    • Click "add featured in" in the upper left corner
    • Enter the corresponding Album name and Artist name
    • Click "Save"
  • Click on "Admin" button below
  • Click on "tracks" table
  • Click "add track" in the upper left corner
  • Fill out the form
  • Enter the Track length in decimal format, e.g. 5.20


  • If you want to add featured artists to the track:
    • Click the link to the "catalog" in the upper right corner
    • Click "featured ins"
    • Click "add featured in" in the upper left corner
    • Enter the corresponding Track name and Artist name
    • Click "Save"
